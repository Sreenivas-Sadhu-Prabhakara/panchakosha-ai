Why "Panchakosha"

Five sheaths, gross to subtle.

In the Vedantic Pancha Kosha model, the self is wrapped in five sheaths — from the tangible body outward-in to a luminous core. Learning AI has the same shape: you start with what you can touch and use, and move inward toward how it truly works and how to create it.

Each level here is one kosha. You don't have to believe the philosophy — it's just an honest map of the journey from using to understanding to making.

  • Annamayathe food/body sheath; here, the tangible → Use
  • Pranamayathe vital-energy sheath; here, fluency → Master
  • Manomayathe mind sheath → Understand
  • Vijnanamayathe wisdom sheath; here, craft → Build
  • Anandamayathe bliss/core sheath → Innovate
