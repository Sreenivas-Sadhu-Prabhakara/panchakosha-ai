🏛️ Level 1 Capstone

Your AI-Powered Week

Prove you can fold AI into real life. Over one week, use an assistant to run four everyday jobs — then keep the result as a portfolio piece you can show anyone.

The goal

Complete four missions below using any AI assistant, saving each prompt-and-result into one document. You'll practise every Level 1 skill: prompting, documents, research, and safe verification.

The four missions

① Feed the week

Plan meals and a grouped shopping list around your real constraints (diet, budget, time). Use a follow-up to swap one meal you don't like.

starter
Plan 5 weeknight dinners for [who], [diet], each under [minutes]. Budget-friendly. Then give a shopping list grouped by supermarket aisle. Ask me one clarifying question first.

② Plan an outing

Pick a real day trip or evening out. Get an itinerary — with sources for opening hours and prices (this practises fact-checking).

starter
Plan a half-day in [place] for [who], budget [amount]. Include opening hours and ticket prices with a source link for each, and flag anything that may have changed recently.

③ Write the hard message

Draft one message you've been putting off — a complaint, a boundary, a request for a raise, a tricky RSVP. Use C-E-F-T; iterate twice on tone.

starter
Context: [who you are, the situation]. Example: [paste a message whose tone you liked, if you have one]. Help me write [the message]. Tone: firm but kind. Format: short. Then give me a softer version and a more direct version so I can choose.

④ Learn one thing

Ask AI to teach you something you've been curious about — with a photo or PDF if relevant — and test your understanding.

starter
Teach me [topic] from scratch in 5 short steps, then quiz me with 3 questions and check my answers. Assume I know nothing.

What to hand in

One document (Google Doc, Notion page, or even a note) titled "My AI-Powered Week" containing, for each mission: the prompt you used, the result, and one line on what you'd change next time.

◆ Success criteria — you've passed when…

✓ Every mission used your real details, not a generic ask.
✓ At least one mission includes a source link you verified.
✓ You iterated at least once (a follow-up that improved the answer).
✓ Nothing sensitive was pasted that you'd regret in a breach.

▸ Stretch goal

Do the whole capstone by voice on your phone. Then ask the assistant to summarise your week's document into a 3-bullet "what AI saved me" recap.
